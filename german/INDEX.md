# German Learning — Knowledge Index

**Primary source:** Hammer's German Grammar and Usage, 5th Ed. (Martin Durrell, Routledge 2011)

## Domains

| Domain | Folder | Covers |
|---|---|---|
| Grammar | [grammar/](grammar/rules.md) | Cases, word order, articles, adjective endings, tenses, passive, subjunctive, conjunctions, prepositions |
| Verbs | [verbs/](verbs/rules.md) | Conjugation patterns, strong/weak/irregular verbs, separable prefixes, modal auxiliaries, valency |
| Vocabulary | [vocabulary/](vocabulary/knowledge.md) | Thematic clusters, collocations, register (formal vs colloquial), false friends, compound patterns |
| Modal Particles | [modal-particles/](modal-particles/knowledge.md) | doch, ja, halt, eben, schon, denn, wohl, mal, eigentlich, etc. |

## How to use
- `rules.md` — apply these by default, they are confirmed
- `knowledge.md` — facts, patterns, examples
- `hypotheses.md` — unclear, context-dependent, needs more exposure to confirm
